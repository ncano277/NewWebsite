Trinity University

Trinity University is located in San Antonio, Texas. The campus is three miles north of downtown San Antonio and the River Walk and six miles south of the San Antonio Airport. The student body consists of approximately 2,300 undergraduate and 200 graduate students. Trinity offers 42 Majors and 57 minors among 6 degree programs and has an endowment of $1.29 billion, the 89th largest in the U.S.[


Photo courtesy of Trinity University

Trinity has a beautiful campus. Trinity campus is known for its 166-Foot Murchison Tower, which previously was the highest point in San Antonio. The campus is beautiful due to its abundance of trees and greenery. When walking around, you will most likely run into one of the trinicats, which can be found all over campus.

The features about Trinity that I appreciate most are the academic rigor and small class sizes. I like the liberal arts aspect of the school that lets me experience learning new things in all areas.


I chose Trinity University because of its academic prestige, as well as its proximity to home for me. More specifically, the type of education, as well as the type of connections i will receive from Trinity can not be matched by any other school in the country.
